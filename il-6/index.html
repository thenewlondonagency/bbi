Interleukin-6 (IL-6) is an important cytokine during the acute phase reaction in response to inflammation, autoimmune and sepsis where the normal serum concentration is significantly elevated in patients.

IL-6 has a wide ranges of uses across a number of diseases and can be used to diagnose sepsis in adults and neonates, monitor infection after surgery, and is positively correlated with the severity of COVID-19.

BBI's IL-6 antibodies outperform competitors and offer superior sensitivity, higher purity and proven performance in ELISA and lateral flow for IVD manufacturers and researchers.
